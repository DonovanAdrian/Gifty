"How do I add a gift to my list?"

By selecting the "Home" tab at the top of the page, you click/tap the "Add Gift" button to add a gift to your list. You will be brought to another page where you can fill out any necessary information that will help others find the gift you are asking for.

This same "Add Gift" button can also be found on your friend's private lists, in which you can add gifts that your friends cannot see.

"How do I invite friends to see my list?"

First and foremost, you will need your friend's user name or their share code in order to invite them, so be sure to have that information ready. By selecting the "# Friends" tab at the top of the page, you can click/tap the "Send Invite" button. A popup will appear where you can provide their user name or share code to invite a friend to your friend list. Once they accept your friend request, their name will be added to the"Gift Lists" page, "Friends" page, and will be able to see your gift list.

"How do I view someone else's gift list?"

By selecting the "Gift Lists" tab at the top of the page, you can see one of the lists of individuals that have accepted your friend requests. Each item in the list will say "View *person*'s Gift Lists". By clicking on one of these items, a popup will appear where you can choose to view one the two types of gift lists... Public and private gift lists. No matter what, the owner of these lists can't see what is bought, but only the public list is curated by the owner (The gifts in the public list are the same gifts that everyone adds on the "Home" tab). The private list, however, is curated by their friends! Therefore, the private list will always remain a mystery to the owner.

"How do I remove a friend from my friend list?"

By selecting the "# Friends" tab at the top of the page, you can see another of the lists of individuals that have accepted your friend requests. If you click on their name, a popup window will appear where you will be shown their public account details. In addition, you can click on "Unfriend" on the bottom right of the popup window to remove that user from your friend list.
